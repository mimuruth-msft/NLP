2015 Used Toyota Tacoma Review

By Auto Auction Mall Team Updated: 09/24/2018 Posted: 09/26/2018

The 2015 Toyota Tacoma is a popular model truck that many consumers love. Overall, this truck ranks an 8.7 out of 10 based on its features, affordability, retained value, and more. It is no secret that the Tacoma holds its value and makes it a wonderful purchase for any consumer who wants a truck that is built tough and will resell at a high value. In fact, Edmunds has ranked the Tacoma as a truck with the best overall resale value throughout the US. Edmunds also projects that the 2015 Tacoma will retain about 52 percent of its original value, which is much higher than some of the luxury brands on the market. Kelley Blue Book also lists the Tacoma as number one in its resale value.

Below, we will look at the 2015 Toyota Tacoma and provide you with a brief review about it. If you are in the market for a used Toyota truck, you will find that this review can help you determine is that used Toyota Tacoma for sale is right for you.

2015 Used Toyota Tacoma Review

The Toyota Tacoma has proved time and time again that it is able to stack up to some of the big dogs out there on the road. The truck itself is built to be durable and take on any terrain that you may find yourself heading towards. The Tacoma can excel over its predecessors and continues to make a mark in the automotive world.

Is a Used Toyota Tacoma Expensive to Own?

One of the notable features of the 2015 Toyota Tacoma is that it has an overall low cost of ownership over a five-year period. In fact, the cost of ownership over this time is about equal to the cost of a Toyota Prius, which is much smaller in size and lacks the same capabilities.

Below, you will find a small cost breakdown of a used 2015 Tacoma over a five-year period.

  • Fuel cost: $9,523
  • Maintenance: $6,201
  • Repairs: $1,841

When it comes to fuel costs, it is important to note that a used 2015 Tacoma will net you an estimated 21 miles per gallon in the city and 25 miles per gallon on the highway.

Does a 2015 Used Tacoma Stack Up in Performance?

One of the factors that may influence your decision to purchase a used Tacoma is the performance of the truck itself. You want a truck that you can rely on for years to come and the Tacoma is that truck. But, how does it stack up?

The 2015 Toyota Tacoma is equipped with a four-cylinder engine that boasts 159 horsepower for you to unleash. If you are looking for a more powerful engine, the option to have a V6, 236 horsepower engine is available. The four-cylinder and V6 engine options both pair with either a manual or automatic transmission.

The Tacoma’s base engine (four cylinder) is perfect for anyone who commutes to and from work or around town. The V6 upgraded engine offers additional power to allow you to haul and tow items around town.

Rear-wheel drive is standard in the 2015 Toyota Tacoma; however, the upgraded models have four-wheel drive. Consumers have rated the ride of the truck as comfortable and smooth.

Is a Used 2015 Toyota Tacoma Safe?

If safety is one of your main concerns in a vehicle, you will find that this truck stacks up. It received a four out of five stars in overall safety. The Tacoma also received a five out of five stars for side impact crashes as well. According to the IIHS, the 2015 Tacoma received a testing of good in the crash areas of head restraints and seats, side, and front. The lowest rating, which was marginal, was roof strength; however, the Tacoma did score a four out of five in rollover crashes for safety.

2015 Used Toyota Tacoma: Is It the Right Choice for You?

If you are eyeing a used 2015 Toyota Tacoma for sale, you may be on the path to the perfect truck for your needs. Each Tacoma is packed with an engine that is ready to perform and with good safety crash test ratings, you will know that you are safe as you drive around town.

The Tacoma has earned its place in the truck lineup and continues to make consumers happy with their choice to purchase one. Consumers who do own this truck has the following to say about it:

  • “Love this truck!”
  • “Awesome midsize truck and meets my needs.”
  • “Perfect for all of my needs.”

The average price of a used 2015 Toyota Tacoma is somewhere between $15,000 and $20,000. This truck is known to hold its value over time, which makes it a great investment.

If you are thinking about a Toyota Tacoma, go test drive one because we are confident that you will fall in love with it!
