Used 2016 BMW 2 Series Review

By Auto Auction Mall Team Updated: 09/24/2018 Posted: 09/27/2018

When it comes to cars, if you are someone who enjoys luxury and comfort while on the road, then a used 2016 BMW 2 Series may be the right choice for you. BMW has taken manufacturing its cars to a whole new level and they focus on the consumer and what you want. Some of the best features that consumers love about the BMW is its ability to ride nice, the high fuel economy it offers, and the style of the interior.

If you want a car that has remained top in its class for a while and won multiple awards, a used BMW 2 series is right up your alley. In fact, the 2016 BMW 2 series won the following:

  • #3 in 2016 Luxury Small Cars
  • #12 in Used Luxury Convertibles $20K and Up
  • #4 in 2016 Luxury Convertibles

Below, we will talk about how the used 2016 BMW 2 stacks up and the purpose of this guide is to help you better understand if this car may be the right choice for you and your family.

How is the Performance on a Used 2016 BMW 2 Series?

The 2016 BMW 2-Series comes with an engine that is ready to take on the black top and push you past everyone else. Many consumers boast that the engine provides them with an ample amount of power.

The standard BMW 2 Series will come with a four-cylinder engine that cranks out about 240 horsepower on average. If you are someone who enjoys a boosted engine, you can choose a six-cylinder that pushes out about 365 horsepower.

Is a Used 2016 BMW 2 Series Affordable?

When purchasing a used 2016 BMW 2 Series, you can expect to pay somewhere between $20,000 and $40,000 for the vehicle. Since there are several models that fall under the 2 Series, the price will vary.

The estimated true cost to own a 2016 BMW 2 Series is $47,547. This number is reached using a five-year estimate with the car being driven about 15,000 miles per year.

To further breakdown the cost over the five-year period, we will look at these numbers:

  • Maintenance Costs: $8,084
  • Fuel: $9,803
  • Repairs: $5,770

Will My Used 2016 BMW 2 Series Be Safe?

The IIHS or Insurance Institute for Highway Safety ranks the safety of cars and provides a breakdown of their crashworthiness. These ratings can help you determine if a car is safe and what you could expect if you were ever involved in an accident.

The IIHS ranked the 2016 BMW 2 good in all areas of crashworthiness. The BMW 2 also ranked advanced in front crash prevention.

Should I Buy a Used 2016 BMW 2 Series?

When evaluating whether the 2016 BMW 2 Series is a good car, you must look at the above covered points and consider the interior, exterior, and overall look and comfort of the car. The BMW 2 series is a sensible choice when it comes to luxury cars and there are several options you can choose from as well.

A used 2016 BMW 2 has superior performance on the road and is easy to control and handle. With awesome features packed into the dashboard and room throughout the cabin, you are sure to enjoy this luxury car.

If you see a used 2016 BMW 2 Series for sale, take a moment and check it out – it will be worth it in the end and this car will provide you with many years of enjoyment.
